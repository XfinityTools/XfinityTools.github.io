The Business Days Calculator tools allows users to calculate the number of working days between two calendar dates. It excludes weekends (Saturdays and Sundays) from the count, providing an accurate total of business days. This tool is helpful for project planning, payroll estimation, and scheduling tasks that depend on weekdays only. It operates directly in the browser without requiring installation.

Business Days Calculator - Instruction Manual

Accessing the Tool:
Open the Business Days Calculator in your web browser. No download or installation is required.

Selecting Dates:
Use the date pickers to select a Start Date and an End Date. Both fields are required to proceed.

Calculating Business Days:
Once both dates are selected, click the "Calculate Business Days" button. The tool will automatically exclude Saturdays and Sundays, and then display the total number of business days between the two selected dates.

Viewing Results:
The result will appear below the form, clearly indicating the number of business days.

Tips & Recommendations:
Ensure that the end date is not earlier than the start date. For best accuracy, use this tool alongside a list of public holidays if needed, since holidays are not excluded by default.

Troubleshooting:
If no result is displayed, verify that both dates are filled in correctly and that the end date follows the start date. Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser.
