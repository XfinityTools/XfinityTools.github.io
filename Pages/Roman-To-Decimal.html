The Roman to Decimal Converter allows users to convert Roman numerals into their standard decimal (Arabic numeral) equivalents. This tool is ideal for students, educators, historians, and anyone interested in number systems or classical references.

Roman to Decimal Converter - Instruction Manual

  • Step 1: Enter a valid Roman numeral in uppercase (e.g., XIV, MMXXV) into the input field.
  • Step 2: Click the Convert button to process the Roman numeral.
  • Step 3: View the equivalent decimal value displayed below the form.
  • Clear: Use the Clear button to reset the form and remove the previous result.
  • Tips: Roman numerals are case-insensitive, but the tool expects them in uppercase. Invalid characters will result in an error.
  • Troubleshooting: Only input valid Roman numerals using the letters I, V, X, L, C, D, and M. Avoid spaces or punctuation.
  • Support: Contact your website administrator or technical support for any issues with the converter.
