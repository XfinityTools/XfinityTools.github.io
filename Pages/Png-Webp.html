This free online tool allows you to convert PNG image files to WebP format quickly and easily. WebP is a modern image format that provides excellent compression and quality, making it perfect for web use.

Instructions:

  • Click the "Choose Files" button or drag and drop PNG files into the designated area.
  • Once the image is uploaded, a preview will appear.
  • Click "Download" to save your converted WebP image to your device.
