The Image Merger Tool is a free online JavaScript-based utility that allows you to combine multiple images (PNG, JPEG, WebP, etc.) into a single merged image. You can choose from three layout options: Horizontal, Vertical, or Grid.


Instructions:

Step 1: Select Images

1. Click inside the upload area to open the file selection dialog.

2. Browse and select the images you want to merge. You can select multiple images at once.

3. Alternatively, you can drag and drop your images into the upload area.

Step 2: Choose Layout

1. Select one of the layout options: Horizontal, Vertical, or Grid.

2. Your choice determines how the final image will be arranged.

Step 3: Merge and Download

1. Click the "Merge Images" button to generate the merged image.

2. Once the merging process is complete, the merged image will be automatically downloaded to your device.


Note: All processing is done locally in your browser. No files are uploaded to any server, ensuring your privacy and speed.
