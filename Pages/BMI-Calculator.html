The BMI (Body Mass Index) Calculator allows users to determine their body mass index using either metric or imperial units. It supports flexible height formats such as feet and inches (e.g., 5'4", 5.4, or 5.3), and provides immediate feedback on BMI classification. This tool is useful for health assessment and monitoring.

BMI Calculator Instruction Manual

  • Select your preferred unit: Metric (kg/cm) or Imperial (lbs/ft+in).
  • Enter your weight:
    • Metric: in kilograms (e.g., 68)
    • Imperial: in pounds (e.g., 150)
  • Enter your height:
    • Metric: in centimeters (e.g., 165)
    • Imperial: in one of the following formats:
      • 5'4"
      • 5′4″ (unicode feet/inches)
      • 5.4 or 5.3 (interpreted as 5 feet and fractional inches)
      • 5 (interpreted as 5 feet, 0 inches)
  • Click the "Calculate BMI" button to get your result.
  • The result will show your BMI value and its category (e.g., Normal, Overweight, Obese).
