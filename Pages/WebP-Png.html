The WebP to PNG Converter is a web-based tool that allows you to convert WebP image files to PNG format. It provides a simple and user-friendly interface where you can either drag and drop WebP files or click to select them from your local system. Once you've selected an image, it will be displayed on a canvas, and you can convert it to PNG format. The converted PNG image can then be downloaded for further use.

Webp to Png Conversion - Instructions

  1. Open the WebP to PNG Converter in your web browser.
  2. You will see a designated area labeled "Drag and drop WebP files here" with a dashed border.
  3. To convert WebP files, you have two options:
    • Drag and drop: Simply locate the desired WebP file(s) on your computer and drag them onto the designated area in the browser window. The area will highlight when you hover over it, indicating that you can release the file(s) to initiate the conversion.
    • Click to select: Click on the designated area, and a file selection dialog will appear. Browse your computer to find the WebP file you wish to convert, and select it. You can repeat this step to select multiple WebP files if needed.
  4. Once you have selected a WebP file, it will be loaded and displayed on the canvas within the browser window. The canvas will resize to fit the dimensions of the image.
  5. After the image is displayed, the "Convert to PNG" button will become enabled.
  6. Click the "Convert" button to convert the WebP image to PNG format.
  7. Once the conversion is complete, a download link labeled "Download" will appear.
  8. Click the "Download" link to save the converted PNG image to your computer.
