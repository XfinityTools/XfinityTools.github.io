The Discount Calculator is a simple and flexible tool designed to calculate any one of three key values: the original price, the discount percentage, or the final price after discount. Users may enter any two of the three values, and the tool will compute the third automatically. It supports both forward and reverse discount calculations and displays results with clear precision.

Discount Calculator - Instruction Manual

  • Step 1: Enter any two of the following values:
    • Original Price
    • Discount Percentage
    • Final Price
  • Step 2: Click the Calculate button. The tool will determine the missing value.
  • Step 3: View the result below the form. It will show the calculated amount and label.
  • Clear Function: Use the Clear button to reset all fields and results.
  • Tips: Ensure only two fields are filled. Leaving more or fewer will result in an error message.
  • Troubleshooting: If you receive a message about incomplete or incorrect input, check that only two fields are filled in and the values are valid numbers.
  • Support: For tool-related issues, please contact your site administrator or support team.
