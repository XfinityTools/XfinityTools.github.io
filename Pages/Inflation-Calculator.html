The Inflation Calculator estimates the future value of an amount of money after accounting for inflation over a number of years. This helps you understand how much prices or costs may increase over time due to inflation.

Inflation Calculator - Instruction Manual

  • Step 1: Enter the initial amount of money you want to evaluate.
  • Step 2: Input the annual inflation rate as a percentage.
  • Step 3: Enter the number of years over which inflation will be applied.
  • Step 4: Click the “Calculate” button.
  • Result: The calculator will display the estimated value of the initial amount after inflation and the increase due to inflation.
  • Clear: Use the “Clear” button to reset the form fields and results.
  • Tips: Use actual inflation rates relevant to your country for accurate estimates.
  • Troubleshooting: Ensure all inputs are non-negative numbers.
  • Support: Contact support if you experience any problems or need assistance.
