The Pythagorean Theorem Calculator helps determine the length of the hypotenuse (side C) in a right-angled triangle when the lengths of the other two sides (A and B) are known. This tool is useful for students, engineers, architects, and anyone working with right triangles.

Pythagorean Theorem Calculator - Instruction Manual

  • Step 1: Enter the lengths of side A and side B (the two shorter sides) in the input fields.
  • Step 2: Click the Calculate Hypotenuse button to find the length of side C (the hypotenuse).
  • Step 3: View the calculated length displayed below the form.
  • Clear: Use the Clear button to reset all inputs and results.
  • Tips: Only positive numbers are accepted. Decimal values are allowed for precision.
  • Troubleshooting: Ensure inputs are not empty and are valid positive numbers.
  • Support: For assistance, contact your technical support or website administrator.
