This tool allows you to convert between different units of fuel efficiency: Miles per Gallon (MPG), Kilometers per Liter (km/L), and Liters per 100 Kilometers (L/100km). It is useful for comparing vehicle fuel consumption figures from different regions and standards. The form accepts a numerical value, the original unit, and the desired unit, and returns the converted result instantly.

Instruction Manual

  • Enter the fuel efficiency value you wish to convert in the "Enter Value" field.
  • Select the unit of the value you entered from the "From" dropdown menu.
  • Select the unit you wish to convert to from the "To" dropdown menu.
  • Click the "Convert" button.
  • The result will be displayed below the form.

Conversions supported:

  • MPG (Miles per Gallon) to km/L or L/100km
  • km/L (Kilometers per Liter) to MPG or L/100km
  • L/100km (Liters per 100 Kilometers) to MPG or km/L
