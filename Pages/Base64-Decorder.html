Decoded Output



The Base64 Decoder converts Base64-encoded text back into its original readable form. This tool is helpful for developers and users who need to decode data embedded in URLs, APIs, or email systems. It supports UTF-8 text decoding and includes a one-click copy feature for convenience.

Base64 Decoder - Instruction Manual

Accessing the Tool:
Open the Base64 Decoder directly in your web browser. No installation or sign-in is required.

Entering Encoded Text:
In the "Enter Base64 Text to Decode" area, paste or type the Base64-encoded string you wish to decode.

Decoding the Text:
Click the "Decode" button. The tool will process the input and display the decoded plain text in the output area below.

Copying the Decoded Output:
After decoding, click the "Copy Output" button to copy the plain text to your clipboard. A confirmation alert will appear once copied.

Tips & Recommendations:
Ensure the Base64 input is correctly formatted. Invalid or corrupted strings may result in an error. This tool is ideal for small to moderate text strings.

Troubleshooting:
If decoding fails, verify the Base64 string is complete and does not contain invalid characters. If the output appears garbled, the original content may not have been properly UTF-8 encoded.
