The GCD and LCM Calculator computes the Greatest Common Divisor (GCD) and Least Common Multiple (LCM) of two positive integers. It is useful for math students and professionals needing quick calculations.

GCD and LCM Calculator - Instruction Manual

  • Step 1: Enter two positive integers in the input fields.
  • Step 2: Click the “Calculate GCD and LCM” button.
  • Step 3: View the results displayed below the form.
  • Clear: Use the “Clear” button to reset inputs and results.
  • Tips: Only positive whole numbers are accepted.
  • Troubleshooting: Ensure valid positive integers are entered.
  • Support: Contact support for technical assistance.
