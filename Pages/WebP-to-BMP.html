Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ):

1. What is a WebP file?

WebP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web.

2. What is BMP format?

BMP (Bitmap) is a widely supported uncompressed image format that preserves image data without loss, ideal for printing or pixel-precise editing.

3. Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All conversions happen locally in your browser. Your image never leaves your device.

4. Can I convert multiple WebP files at once?

Yes. This tool currently supports Bulk conversion.

5. Does this tool work on mobile devices?

Yes. The tool is mobile-friendly and works on most modern smartphones and tablets.
