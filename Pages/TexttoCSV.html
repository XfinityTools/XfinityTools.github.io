Text to CSV Converter

Description: This tool allows you to convert text data into a CSV format, which is commonly used for data import/export in spreadsheet applications. Simply enter your data in the provided text area, format it with rows separated by line breaks and columns separated by commas, and download it as a CSV file.

Instructions:

  • Step 1: Enter your data into the text area. Each row of data should be on a new line, and columns should be separated by commas. For example: 
    Name, Age, Location
    John Doe, 30, New York
    Jane Smith, 25, Los Angeles
  • Step 2: Click the "Download as CSV" button to generate the CSV file.
  • Step 3: The file will be saved as output.csv, which you can open in a spreadsheet program.
